    About

    The company started its work from the developments in the packaging industry and extended it to the fields of textile, soap and washing powder, thermoforming, food engineering, bakery and beverages, printing, Disposable items, bag making and shoe making, and large scale bigger production. We’ve made so many challenging machines and delivered new ideas of automation to all these industries.

    Simplification & ease in the work is the hallmark of our company

    We have reduced & shortened very complex and junk mechanical systems in various machines even upto ten times than their conventional designs.

    • Another remarkable service we provide is the competition of hundreds of research projects of the students and research scholars of the universities of the province.
    • We also provide the services of consultancy for the challenging technical problems to the industry. We are on the panel of Master Group of Industries, Pepsico, Rafhan Maiz Products, Dairy Lac Feeds, Vived Packages, Rehmat Flour Mills, Agri Tourism Corporation and among others.
    • We've restored & upgraded many medical equipments of DHQ & Allied Hospitals volantarily. In the pendamic of COVID-19 we've served the nation vigorously by providing the solutions of sanitising and face-mask making. Recently we are working for the facilitation of the formers by making automatic jaggery production unit which is the need of the time.
